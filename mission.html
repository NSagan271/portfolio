Mission Statement

To become an inspiration to others. To set an example for the next generation. To be a lifelong learner and use my knowledge to improve society. To enrich people's lives through my work as a programmer and try my hardest at anything. To help fight for equality. To be a leader and focus on what's best for the world. To bring happiness to those around me. To be kind to others no matter what. To persevere through even the most difficult of struggles, and come out of those struggles stronger. To learn from my mistakes. To never give up and never back down from a task. To do something worthwhile with my life and accomplish something truly great. To stand up for what I believe in, even against society, and have strong morals and values.
