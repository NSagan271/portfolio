I am a junior at San Leandro High School (SLHS), and I have a passion for programming. I aspire to be a software engineer. Ever since the beginning of my high school experience, I have worked on projects in several programming languages (including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, and C++), both as a collaborative effort and individually. As a part of the programming club Hack Club SLHS, I have attended 4 hackathons so far over the past two years and helped develop many prototypes for Android and Web applications. My favorites include a simple Web game (available here), as well as a quiz app for SAT vocabulary words and an application to help people make choices. Also, I currently have an Internship with the company Magnitude.io involving the design, building, and programming of an air quality monitoring station.
In addition to programming, I am also involved in other activities, including running cross country, playing saxophone in the school's jazz band, and being a member of a writing club at SLHS. I enjoy playing and listening to music, writing short stories, drawing, reading, and designing games. In whatever I do, I am devoted to learning and doing my best.

My Goals

  • Personal:

    • Short term: to become more comfortable in social situations, including those involving public speaking.

    • Long term: to become more confident with who I am as a person.

  • Educational:

    • Short term: to get the most out of my high school experience by taking and doing my best on all of the AP courses that interest me.

    • Long term: to be accepted into a good college and learn skills that I need for employment as a software developer.

  • Professional:

    • Short term: to successfully complete the production of an air quality monitor during my internship with Magnitude.io and continue my work with that company.

    • Long term: to have a successful career in the computer science or engineering field.

My Philosophy on Life

My philosophy on life is to always try your hardest because the worst thing you can do in most situation is give up or not try at all. If you do your best and learn as much as you can, it is possible to avoid failure.
