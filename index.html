I am a junior at San Leandro High School (SLHS), and I have a passion for programming. I aspire to be a software engineer. Ever since the beginning of my high school experience, I have worked on projects in several programming languages, both as a collaborative effort and individually. As a part of the programming club Hack Club SLHS, I have attended 4 hackathons so far over the past two years and helped develop many prototypes for Android and Web applications. My favotites include a quiz app for SAT vocabulary words and an application to help people make choices, both created as a team effort, and a Web game (available here). Also, I currently have an Internship with the company Magnitude.io involving the design, building, and programming of an air quality monitoring station.
In addition to programming, I am also involved in other activities, including running cross country, playing saxophone in the school's jazz band, and being a member of a writing club at SLHS.
